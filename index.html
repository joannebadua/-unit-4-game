Try to match your carbon footprint score with the goal carbon footprint score by recycling!

Click on one of the four recycling bins until the scores match. By clicking on a bin, you will add a specific amount of carbon footprint points.

to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the goal carbon footprint score. You lose the game if your total score goes above the goal number.

The value of each bin is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each recycling bin, and a brand new goal carbon footprint score.
